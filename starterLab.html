Who Are We?

We are design lab. Our main goal is to prove that computer science and design are very good friends. Instead of just learning coding, we believe that a good knowledge in design can really help computer science students to think outside of the box. Design is everywhere, just think about it. In our lab we will focus on adobe products and use them for web and app development fields. And we will learn the basics of Ux/Ui design so that we can make user friendly products. We believe that it is all about way of thinking.
